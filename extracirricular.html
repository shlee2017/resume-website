University of Michigan Taekwondo Club

White Belt Instructor

Michigan Taekwondo Club is a student-run club sport under the Rec Sports department. In our club you will learn the same basics that are taught in typical taekwondo dojangs, but the sport and athletic aspects of taekwondo are emphasized. As the White Belt Instructor, I am in charge of teaching the White Belts the basic fundamental of Taekwondo. I either lead class or do a one on one tutoring to those students that are struggling.

Time Involved: September 2017 - Present

Learn more: https://www.umtaekwondo.club

University of Michigan Taekwondo Club Demonstration Team

Dance Portion Leader

The University of Michigan Taekwondo Club Demonstration Team is a Demonstration Team that showcase Taekwondo, a Korean martial art, is like by performing at various events across campus. As the Dance Portion Leader of the team, I am in charge of the dance portion of our Taekwondo Performance. I choreograph the dance mixed with Korean Martial Art and teach it to other members. I also assist on choreographing other parts of the performance such as fighting scences, breaking boards, and the overall layout and concept of the performance.

Time Involved: September 2017 - Present

Society of Asian Scientists and Engineers

General Member

The Society of Asian Scientists and Engineers is a nation-wide organization with the mission of providing opportunities for scientists and engineers of Asian heritage to enhance their professional skills and to connect them with industry-leading companies. Our chapter aims to create a community of students at Michigan, and act as a gateway to workshops, volunteering events, company information sessions and much more.

Time Involved: September 2018 - Present

Michigan Muscle Club

General Member

The Michigan Muscle Club (MMC) is the fastest growing U of M fitness organization dedicated to improving the wellness of all students. We are a fitness group that welcomes all levels of experience, genders, orientations, ethnicities, etc. One of the most prominent features of MMC is its 7 AM challenge. Many MMC members arrive at CCRB at 7 AM every weekday and work out together in groups consisting of mentors and mentees. We provide a supportive environment that motivates our members to wake up early and exercise.

Time Involved: September 2018 - Present

Learn more: maizepages.umich.edu/organization/mmc

Harvest Missionn Community Church

General Member

Harvest Mission Community Church began in 1996 with a group of eight people and a dream: to be a 1st century church in the 21st century. As we looked into the book of Acts, God gave us the vision to be a community of faith that raises bold disciples of Christ who will transform the world. We planted our roots on the University of Michigan campus in Ann Arbor with a focus of reaching college students, because we believe that college is a pivotal time for people to discover their God-given destiny.

Time Involved: September 2017 - Present
